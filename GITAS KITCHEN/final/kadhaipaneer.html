Kadai Paneer

Kadai Paneer – paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and bell peppers cooked in a spicy masala. This restaurant style Kadai Paneer gets its unique flavor from freshly ground spices like coriander and red chilies! Enjoy it with naan or paratha. Kadai Paneer is a popular paneer recipe where paneer and bell peppers are cooked in a spicy masala. It is one of the most popular paneer recipes. It’s there on the menu in almost all Indian restaurants. I remember always ordering it when we ate out. Kadai is a like a wok commonly used in Indian cooking. We use it to make stir fry and all sorts of things. We also use it for deep frying snacks and sweets. This dish is called Kadai Paneer because it’s cooked in a kadai. But if you don’t have a kadai you can simply use a wok or any other pan. I actually did not make it in a kadai!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desserts

Directions:

Make Kadai Masala

step 1

Add all the ingredients to a small skillet.

step 2

Roast on medium heat until lightly browned and fragrant (3-4 minutes).

step 3

Remove the skillet from the heat and let the spices cool slightly.

step 4

Add them to a grinder or a food processor and grind to make a smooth powder. Your kadai masala is ready.

Make Kadai Paneer

step 5

Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a pan over high heat.

step 6

Once the oil is hot, add cubed onions and cubed green bell peppers and saute until they are slightly charred (3-4 minutes). We want them to be slightly crunchy.

step 7

Remove on a plate and set aside.

step 8

In the same pan, add the remaining 3 tablespoons of oil and heat it on medium-high heat.

step 9

When the oil is hot, add chopped onions and fry until they are lightly browned (4-5 minutes).

step 10

Add green chilies and ginger-garlic paste and fry until onions turn golden brown in color (6-8 minutes). Keep stirring frequently while frying.

step 11

Now add tomato puree and chopped tomatoes and fry for 2-3 minutes.

step 12

Add the ground kadai masala powder, salt, Kashmiri red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Add half a cup of water and cook for 2-3 minutes.

step 13

Finally, add paneer and the sauteed onions and bell peppers and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add some more water if the gravy is too thick.

step 14

Cook until oil starts to separate from the sides (2-3 minutes). Add ghee, and lime juice and mix well. Serve hot.

ingredients

Make Kadai Paneer

2 tablespoons whole coriander seeds

3-4 whole Kashmiri dry red chilies

1 teaspoon fennel seeds

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

2-3 cloves

2 whole black cardamoms

5-6 whole black peppercorns

1 inch piece of cinnamon stick

For The Curry

Add green chilies and ginger-garlic paste and fry until onions turn golden brown in color (6-8 minutes). Keep stirring frequently while frying.

Add the ground kadai masala powder, salt, Kashmiri red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Add half a cup of water and cook for 2-3 minutes.
